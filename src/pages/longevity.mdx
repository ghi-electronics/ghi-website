# Longevity

Many embedded products remain in production for **10 years or more**. At GHI Electronics, we design and manufacture our platforms in-house, enabling support for customers with extended product lifecycles and predictable upgrade paths.

When platforms evolve, we aim to provide **drop-in or software-compatible replacements** so your products can migrate with minimal redesign.

![GHI Electronics product timeline](/img/longevity-timeline.jpg)

Because GHI Electronics designs and manufactures its hardware internally, we maintain control over component sourcing and production. This allows us to extend product availability, provide compatible replacements, and manage end-of-life transitions responsibly.
