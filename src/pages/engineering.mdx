# Engineering

## Full-Scale Engineering

GHI Electronics helps companies design, develop, and validate embedded products from concept to production-ready hardware.

![Product life cycle diagram](/img/product-life-cycle.png)

### System Architecture

We define system requirements and design overall product architecture, optimizing hardware and software approaches for performance, cost, and manufacturability.

### Schematic Design

Our hardware engineers convert concepts into schematics with the necessary components based on client requirements.

### PCB Layout

We design and route printed circuit boards with emphasis on signal integrity, power distribution, manufacturability, and long-term reliability.

### Prototype Build and Bring-Up

We manufacture and assemble prototype hardware, then perform initial board validation of power, interfaces, and core functionality.

### Firmware Development

Our software engineers assist with designing applications for IoT and embedded designs that contain software components.

### Production Preparation

We prepare designs for manufacturing, including documentation, test procedures, and production support for scaling.
