## Designed for embedded product development

A system-on-module mounts onto a custom carrier board designed for the final product. The carrier provides connectors, sensors, power, and application-specific interfaces — the module brings the compute.

**Common applications:**

- Industrial automation systems
- IoT gateways and connected devices
- Smart kiosks and terminals
- Monitoring and control systems
- Custom embedded electronics
