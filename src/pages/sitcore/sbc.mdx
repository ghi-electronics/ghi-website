## Maker-friendly form factors

The SBC category is designed for compatibility with the most-used maker boards on the market. That compatibility lets you prototype with thousands of available accessories — without giving up commercial-grade silicon.

## SBCs as SoMs

Several boards have castellated holes (edge pads) — the same mounting style as the SMT SoMs. Worth considering when researching SoM options, especially for the SC13048 chipset.
