## When SoCs make sense

The chip-level option is the right choice when:

- You need maximum control over your hardware design
- You want to minimize cost and board size
- Your engineering team is comfortable designing the full embedded hardware

All SITCore SoCs integrate the processor, memory, peripherals, and security features required to build a complete embedded system. They run TinyCLR OS with .NET and C# while remaining compatible with traditional embedded workflows.
