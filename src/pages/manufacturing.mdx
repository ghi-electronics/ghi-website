# Manufacturing

## From Prototype to Production — Built in the USA

GHI Electronics offers comprehensive embedded manufacturing services including assembly, testing, and production support for commercial electronic products.

![Manufacturing cycle diagram](/img/manufacturing-cycle.png)

### Surface-Mount Technology

We use high-precision solder jet-printers and SMT pick-and-place equipment capable of placing ultra-fine-pitch integrated circuits for reliable, scalable production.

### Through-Hole Technology

Automated selective-solder machines support through-hole and mixed-technology assembly, providing robust mechanical and electrical connections.

### Inspection & Quality Control

Detailed inspection and validation procedures — including X-ray analysis and automated testing — ensure manufacturing quality and reliability.

### Environmental & Protective Coating

Conformal coating and protective finishes are applied to enhance durability in harsh or industrial environments.

### Prototype to Production Scaling

The facility transitions seamlessly from prototype builds to mid-volume production while maintaining consistent quality control.

### Testing & Validation

In-house testing of assembled products validates electrical functionality, system stability, and production readiness before deployment.
