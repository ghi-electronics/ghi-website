## The challenge

Traffic infrastructure runs outdoors for years. Each installation is different — different signaling rules, different sensors, different integrations with municipal systems. Vepel needed a hardware and software platform that could absorb that variability without forcing a custom electrical design for every project.

## The solution

Vepel builds on SITCore. The platform handles the outdoor-grade hardware foundation and the secure connectivity layer; Vepel's engineering team adapts the application logic — signaling patterns, sensor integration, communication protocols — to whatever the customer's installation calls for.

## Why SITCore

Two things stood out to Vepel: the secure IoT connectivity story, and the speed of development from a platform that already has the hard parts solved. They can ship something tailored to a specific intersection or signaling regime without rebuilding the foundation for each project.
