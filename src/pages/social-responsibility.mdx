# Social Responsibility

## BrainPad

As engineers, GHI Electronics recognizes education's importance. The company developed the BrainPad to offer an intuitive STEM/STEAM learning experience for students across all age groups.

The platform delivers interactive engagement — both minds-on and hands-on — in classroom settings, helping boost student interest in Science, Technology, Engineering, Art & Mathematics.

Organizations GHI has partnered with include:

- Midtown Mosaic Church LMTLESS summer program
- MACUL CS Summit & Student Technology Showcase at the Capitol
- National Association of Manufacturers' Manufacturing Day
- Maker Faire
- Square One STEM educational network
- Roseville High School REST program
- Code313
- Oakland Schools STEM Summer Camp
- Google coding event from 2017
- Microsoft's DigiGirlz program

[Visit BrainPad Website](https://www.brainpad.com/)

## BeagleBoard.org

![BeagleBone Wireless](/img/beaglebone-wireless.png)

Since 2016, GHI Electronics has served as a design and manufacturing partner for the nonprofit BeagleBoard.org Foundation. Both organizations share a commitment to open-source hardware and software, as well as education. They collaborate on product roadmap discussions and strategic planning.

[Visit BeagleBoard.org Website](https://beagleboard.org/)
