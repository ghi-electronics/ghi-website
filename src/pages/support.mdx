# Support

## Support for Developers and Product Teams

We provide documentation, developer resources, and direct support to help engineers build products using GHI Electronics' platforms.

Whether you're evaluating hardware, developing firmware, or deploying commercial products, our support resources aim to help your team move forward quickly.

[Talk to an engineer →](/contact/)

## Full Technical Documentation

Comprehensive guides, hardware references, and software documentation are available for all GHI Electronics platforms at [docs.ghielectronics.com](https://docs.ghielectronics.com/).

**Quick resources:**

- **Experimenter Kit & eBook** — The *C# for IoT and Robotics* eBook pairs with the SITCore Experimenter Kit and serves as a reference for all SITCore boards. [Experimenter Kit](https://www.ghielectronics.com/experiment/)
- **Pinout** — Full details on SITCore SBCs and pinout/schematics: [SITCore SBC docs](https://docs.ghielectronics.com/hardware/sitcore/sbc.html)
- **Getting Started Docs** — The complete TinyCLR Getting Started guide: [TinyCLR Getting Started](https://docs.ghielectronics.com/software/tinyclr/getting-started.html)
- **Tutorials** — TinyCLR Tutorials cover IoT specifics. Non-specific topics like threading typically work identically to full .NET. [TinyCLR Tutorials](https://docs.ghielectronics.com/software/tinyclr/tutorials/intro.html)
