4iPlatform builds industrial IoT and automation solutions on GHI Electronics hardware. Their systems collect production data directly from machinery on the plant floor, turning raw machine data into reliable operational information.

{/* truncate */}

**4iDASH** gathers real-time data from production lines, enabling managers to make decisions based on actual output rather than estimates.

Their **PLT (Position Tracking System)** continuously tracks the position and status of ladles and heavy equipment in metals manufacturing. The **WMS (Warehouse Management System)** enables real-time inventory management and asset tracking from the plant floor. An **RFID Reader** ties into both PLT and WMS workflows.

Learn more at [4iplatform.com](https://4iplatform.com).
